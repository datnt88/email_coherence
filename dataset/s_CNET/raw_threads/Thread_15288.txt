6803	5	91176	1389991	1389960	linkit	8/29/05 2:44 am	1.12528344e12	upgrade to usb 2.0	you need to open your computer's case to find out if you have an empty pci slot. your usb 2.0 card will come in the form of a pci card that you insert into an unused pci slot in your computer's motherboard. after you insert the card, start up your computer. you need to install the software driver that came with the card. if the card came with no driver, windows will find your card and may try to install a software driver for you. if you need a driver, go to the card manufacturer's website and download a driver to install. the best thing to do is follow the instructions that come with your pci usb 2.0 card.
