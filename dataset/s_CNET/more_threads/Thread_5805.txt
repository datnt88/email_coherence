10720	2	278178	2668059	2667898	ramarc	1/2/08 12:06 pm	1.19927556e12	dual video for what purpose?	if you just want 3 or more monitors, then practically any recent mobo will work if the second card is pci or pci-express x1. for two pci-express cards, you just need a mobo that has two pci-express x16 slots. if you're planning on combining both cards for better gaming performance, then your mobo choice is dictated by your graphics cards. nvidia graphics cards require an sli mobo based on nvidia chipsets. ati graphics require a crossfire mobo based on intel p35/x38 chipsets or an ati chipset. newegg.com has excellent search tools that will help you find suitable mobos.
