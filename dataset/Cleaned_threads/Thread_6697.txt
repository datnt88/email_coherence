<s> optical drives are 5 25 quot </s>
<s> hard drives are 3 5 quot </s>
<s> they both have side mounting screws optical drives have fine thread hard drives have standard thread </s>
<s> some drives both optical and hard are mounted with screws underneath </s>
<s> other drives are screwless using sliding locking bars </s>
<s> only you can tell which you have and this takes eyeballs inside the case </s>
