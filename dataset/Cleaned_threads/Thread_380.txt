<s> i use 2 samsung 213t monitors on a radeon 1600 it really helps to have 2 monitors the same size for applications that spread across the desktop </s>
<s> the dual monitor set up can be done in a couple ways </s>
<s> i purchased a card that would support 2 monitors there are several cards that support 2 monitors </s>
<s> this can also be done with one agp pci express card and a second pci card </s>
<s> in the case of tv tuner cards they typically will require a second card for a dual monitor set up </s>
<s> i use a program call ultramon </s>
<s> this gives a task bar for each monitor a button to send a window to the other monitor a button to expand a window to the desktop wallpaper tools monitor set up tools and other goodies </s>
<s> it is a real handy reliable program </s>
<s> it has a trail version for free and is registered for a price </s>
<s> i originally used the set up for generating reports based on spreadsheats </s>
<s> it worked very well to have the document on one screen and the graphs calculations on the other </s>
<s> excel other ms office programs and any general program require two instances one for each monitor </s>
<s> it is also really good for web surfing im video chat and movie playback </s>
<s> you can keep the windows separate and readable </s>
<s> i have noticed that with dual monitors i can overload the processor much easier due to the extra tasks </s>
<s> i use my machine for audio recording and encoding music playback and games at the same time </s>
<s> i would recommend a robust processor or extra memory with a dual monitor set up to avoid too much lag </s>
